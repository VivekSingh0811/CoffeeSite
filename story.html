Our Story

We Didn't Just
Open A Brand.

We declared war on mediocre coffee.

"It started with one bad cup at an airport in 2008. We swore it would never happen again."

BREWED was born from obsession. Not the startup kind — the kind where you spend months in Yirgacheffe learning to hand-sort green beans at origin, because you genuinely cannot sleep knowing how much potential gets wasted.

Today we source from 23 farms across 11 countries. Every relationship is direct trade. Every roast is small-batch. Every cup that leaves our facility carries the fingerprint of someone who stayed up too late getting it right.

We don't do bulk. We don't do stale. We don't do mediocre. Full stop.

From Soil To Cup

01

We Visit The Farms

Every origin we carry, we've visited. We know the farmers by name. We pay above fair-trade rates — always — because we believe the best coffee comes from trust, not transactions.

02

We Roast Fresh

Roasted to order, shipped the next day. Our internal rule: if it sat longer than a week, it doesn't ship. Our customers deserve what comes out of the roaster — not a warehouse shelf.

03

We Never Compromise

If a harvest isn't exceptional that year, we don't carry it. We'd rather have a smaller catalogue and an unblemished reputation than a full shelf and one disappointed customer.
